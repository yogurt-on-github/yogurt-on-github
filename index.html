Tim Mitchell

Tim Mitchell is an online educator and course developer. He guides people from around the world in courses that are lively, collaborative, and student-focused.

Tim leads courses about information technology, business, and education for the University of Victoria and Thompson Rivers University.

He is also a senior writer with the British Columbia government. He won the National Magazine Award for his fiction.

  • build a relational database

Strategists

  • predict how society will be transformed by technologies such as data mining, cloud computing, and RFID
  • pinpoint risks to privacy, security, and freedom
  • imagine new opportunities for online communities and social production
Educators
  • build skills for teaching and training online
  • enrich courses with Web 2.0 tools
  • promote student collaboration and social learning

Executives

  • align business strategies and technology
  • evaluate metrics to improve business processes
  • plan ways to deliver more value to customers
